Turn location data into business value quickly, efficiently and at scale – with the world's #1 location data platform.

Contact us Get started for free

Everything you need to build location-based solutions faster

Whatever your location-related use case, make it happen with HERE. Our location data and technology platform empowers you to turn location data into business value quickly, efficiently and at scale.

Find high-value curated map content, rich location data and services, advanced tools and infrastructure for developers, a global ecosystem for collaboration, and professional services and support – all in one fully integrated platform.

Precise location data, easily accessible

Live location data in 2D/3D for 200 countries with 1000+ detailed attributes providing a digital representation of reality – delivered in standard formats to simplify development.

Location data processing and analytics at scale

Speed up development with enterprise-grade location data processing, including a full stack of analytics and ML algorithms. Enable faster insights with templates for automation and a low-code/no-code approach.

Configurable services for tailored solutions

Configure our location services with your data and business insights, and build differentiated solutions. Integrate with ease into your application or solution with standardized APIs and SDKs.

Platform capabilities

Visualize data

Visualize static and dynamic location-centric data, building interactive maps with full customization of style and color. Create insightful dashboards or share compelling visual stories with your audience.

Generate insights

Uncover and share location data patterns, trends and relationships revealing actionable insights to inform decisions – and make predictions in real-time with our analytics suite.

Build applications

Build location intelligence into your web and mobile applications, using our map and location content, APIs and SDKs. Build once and operate at scale across geographies.

Develop services

Develop your solution faster using our highly configurable and scalable location services – such as geocoding, routing, rendering, transit and positioning.

Make maps

Create custom maps of private sites and connect them to public maps for seamless search, routing and navigation. Add your business insights to map layers to create custom applications and services.

Monetize assets

Share or monetize your own location-centric offerings or source new assets – such as datasets, APIs, SDKs and algorithms. It's simple and secure with the dedicated HERE Marketplace on our platform.

Three easy steps to using data with the HERE platform

Whether you're building a business app or service or creating a digital map, you'll tend to work with data in three ways – sourcing, processing and usage. With our platform, you typically go through these steps or you can skip some – depending on your project and end goal.

Source the location data you need

Kick-off your project by bringing your own data or building it using our tools in a secure environment. You can also buy curated datasets from the HERE Marketplace.

Process data with location context

Enrich your data with location context. Use our algorithms and pipelines to clean, convert and combine data.

Use the enriched asset

Publish your enriched asset within your organization, or use it as a data catalog, in an SDK, location service or for analytics. Or you can sell it on the HERE Marketplace.
